WELCOME TO

SALMON RIVER

Salmon River Adventures is based in the beautiful Riggins, Idaho. We offer world-class, whitewater rafting experiences on the Salmon River in the middle of the expansive Idaho wilderness. overnight trips for groups and individuals.

JOIN US TODAY!

COME AND ADVENTURE WITH US

The Salmon is at once remote and wild in its setting and idyllic in its character. Its flow is defined by large roller-coaster rapids and intermittent stretches of serene flat-water punctuated by deep green pools and inspiring canyon walls.
